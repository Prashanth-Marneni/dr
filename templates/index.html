Symptoms

Blurred vision

If you feel your vision is getting blurred and you are diabetic patient then there is a possibility of having DR

Spots or dark strings floating in your vision (floaters)

If there are spots in your vision there is a possibility of having DR

Dark or empty areas in your vision

If you are unable to see clearly then there is a possibility of having DR

Precautions

Manage your diabetes

Make healthy eating and physical activity part of your daily routine. Try to get at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity, such as walking, each week.

Monitor your blood sugar level
